初めてのドイツの旅は、フランクフルトで開催されたブックフェア『FRANKFURTER BUCHMESSE』の参加と、ベルリンに短期滞在中の写真家を訪問することが目的。ドイツに行ったことのある（住んでいる）知り合いはクリエイターの割合が多く、そんな人たちを魅了する街にずっと興味があった。私は『快適・安全』に重点を置いて旅をする。海外旅行には刺激と緊張感がつきものだが、スリリングすぎる体験を望まない私にとってドイツは心地よく過ごせて、アートや文化の空気を深くゆっくりと感じられた。

The purpose of my first trip to Germany was to attend the book fair "Frankfurter Buchmesse" in Frankfurt and to visit a photographer who was in Berlin for a short stay. Many among the people I know who have been to (and live in) Germany are creators, and I have always been interested in the city because it attracts such people. I travel with an emphasis on comfort and safety. Traveling abroad is always exciting and nerve-wracking, but for me, who does not want an overly thrilling experience, Germany was a comfortable place to stay and I was able to feel the atmosphere of art and culture slowly and thoroughly.

シンプルで直線的なデザインは、他のキャリーケースと容易に差別化が出来て、すぐにコンベアから見つけることが出来た。そして、頑丈なので余計な心配をしなくていいのが頼もしい。

The simple, linear design easily differentiates mine from other cases, and I was able to find it easily on the conveyor belt. And it is sturdy, so that means I can count on not having to worry unnecessarily.

移動の際は、カメラとこのパスポートケースだけ持って街を回った。カードはもちろん、現地で見たライブのチケット、大量のレシートまで全てをまとめて収納できるのが嬉しい。

When moving around town, I took only my camera and this passport case with me. It is great to be able to store everything together, not only cards, but also tickets to shows I saw in while I was there, and even a large amount of receipts.

滞在中は写真家が借りているアパートメントに宿泊。キャリーケースの仕切りパネルがウォールポケットとして使えて、散らかしがちな充電ケーブル類や、洗顔のミニボトルなどをまとめられて便利だった。

During my stay, I stayed in an apartment rented by a photographer. The divider panel of the case can be used as a wall pocket, which was convenient for organizing charging cables, mini bottles of face wash, and other items that tend to get scattered around.

いつも空港に着いたら、靴からサンダルに履き替えて身軽な移動に。xzeroのサンダルは薄くて丈夫で、乗り継ぎの空港内での移動も全く苦にならない。

I always change from shoes to sandals when I arrive at the airport so that I can travel comfortably. xzero sandals are thin and sturdy, and moving around an airport in transit is not difficult at all.

環境音を録音するためにshure のマイクを持って行った。スマートフォンに直接接続するもので、非常にコンパクト。

I took a shure microphone to record sounds around me. It connects directly to a smartphone and is very compact.

仕事柄、海外旅行に行った際に本屋を回りすぎて、ついつい本を買い過ぎてしまう。そのため大容量で丈夫なキャリーケース選びが重要になる。帰国の際はとても重くなったが、重さを感じさせないスムーズな足回りに感動。

Because of my job, I tend to visit many bookstores when I travel abroad and I end up buying too many books. Therefore, it is important to choose a sturdy, large-capacity case. It was very heavy when I returned home, but I was impressed with its smooth wheels that made it feel light.
